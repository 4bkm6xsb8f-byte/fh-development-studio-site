Discovery Workflow

A checklist workflow for discovery and scoping.

This page is a working reference for the full discovery process, from intake through recommendations, scope alignment, and handoff into build planning.

Overview

Use this checklist to keep scoping clear, complete, and accountable.

The workflow is intentionally not connected to any backend yet. It exists as a structured planning surface the upcoming admin app can adopt.

1. Intake and Initial Framing

Capture the basics before discussing solutions so the recommendation starts from the real problem and constraints.

2. Users, Platforms, and Product Context

Make sure the team understands who the product serves and where it needs to perform well.

3. Scope Definition

Translate the problem into a practical first-release scope before design and build recommendations are made.

4. Risk and Recommendation Review

Pressure-test the plan before the work shifts into estimates, sequencing, and execution.

5. Handoff Into Delivery Planning

Close discovery by making the decision trail clear enough for design, development, or admin workflow follow-up.
