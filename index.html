Featured Product

A studio that builds real products, solves real problems, and understands what users notice first.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck gives users a cleaner way to monitor critical websites, review status fast, and act without digging through noisy dashboards or bloated tooling. It is designed to make monitoring feel calmer, faster, and more useful when uptime visibility actually matters.

  • See everything at a glance: a focused list view shows monitored sites, recent check timing, freshness, and current online or offline status.
  • Take action quickly: users can open details, run a fresh check, review history, copy the URL, edit the site entry, or jump directly into Safari.
  • Stay organized: settings support grouping, monitoring behavior, and simple controls that keep the app readable instead of overloaded.
  • Understand every status: detailed screens show the latest check, freshness, monitoring mode, notification state, and the specific status message behind the result.

Featured App

FHCams gives multi-property owners a cleaner, faster way to stay connected to every camera that matters.

FHCams

FHCams is built for users who need quick visibility across homes, offices, cabins, and other monitored spaces without getting buried in a cluttered camera interface. The app keeps live views, property filters, and settings organized so monitoring stays practical as locations, cameras, and daily demands grow.

  • Move between properties with less friction: filter the grid by location and keep the right cameras visible without digging through menus.
  • See high-value feeds faster: a focused overview helps users scan live views, snapshots, and camera status in one place.
  • Manage growth without chaos: refresh timing, background behavior, and property setup stay readable as more locations and cameras are added.
