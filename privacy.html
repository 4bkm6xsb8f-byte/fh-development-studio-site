Privacy Policy

Clear and minimal data practices.

FH Development Studio aims to explain data practices in plain language and avoid collecting more information than is needed for product functionality.

Overview

This policy applies to FH Development Studio websites and applications unless a product includes a separate, product-specific privacy notice.

Data Collection

FH Development Studio is designed around minimal data handling. Unless explicitly stated within a product, we do not collect personal information on company-operated external servers.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck stores website URLs entered by the user locally on the device to provide the app’s functionality. The app is intended to operate without sending that user-managed monitoring list to company-operated servers.

Third-Party Services

FHWebCheck does not use third-party advertising networks. If a future product version adds analytics, cloud sync, or another external service, this policy should be updated to describe that change clearly.

User Control

Users can manage or remove stored URLs within the app. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to device behavior, operating system settings, and backup configuration.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact support@fhdevelopmentstudio.com.
