Privacy Policy

Clear and minimal data practices.

FH Development Studio aims to explain data practices in plain language and avoid collecting more information than is needed for product functionality.

Overview

This policy applies to FH Development Studio websites and applications unless a product includes a separate, product-specific privacy notice.

Information You Share With Us

If you contact FH Development Studio by email, we may receive the information you choose to include, such as your name, email address, company name, project details, screenshots, and support notes. That information is used only to respond to your inquiry, provide support, or evaluate a potential engagement.

Data Collection

FH Development Studio is designed around minimal data handling. Unless explicitly stated within a product, we do not collect personal information on company-operated external servers.

How Information Is Used

Information shared through support or project inquiries may be used to answer questions, troubleshoot issues, improve product behavior, and communicate about requested services. FH Development Studio does not sell personal information.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck stores website URLs entered by the user locally on the device to provide the app’s functionality. The app is intended to operate without sending that user-managed monitoring list to company-operated servers.

Third-Party Services

FHWebCheck does not use third-party advertising networks. If a future product version adds analytics, cloud sync, or another external service, this policy should be updated to describe that change clearly.

Website and Hosting Considerations

Like most websites, basic technical information such as browser type, device type, referrer details, or server request data may be processed by the hosting platform or infrastructure providers to keep the site available and secure. FH Development Studio does not use this site as a vehicle for invasive tracking.

User Control

Users can manage or remove stored URLs within the app. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to device behavior, operating system settings, and backup configuration.

Data Retention

Support emails and inquiry messages may be retained for a reasonable period to maintain support continuity, document prior decisions, and respond to follow-up requests. Information is not kept longer than needed for those purposes.

Children’s Privacy

FH Development Studio services are not intentionally directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website or our general support channels.

Policy Updates

This policy may be revised when products, services, or data practices change. Updates will be reflected on this page using the last updated date shown below.

Questions

For privacy questions, contact support@fhdevelopmentstudio.com.
