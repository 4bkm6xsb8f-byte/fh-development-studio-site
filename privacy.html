Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. FH Development Studio aims to keep data practices clear, minimal, and easy to understand.

Data Collection

FH Development Studio builds applications designed to minimize data collection. Unless explicitly stated inside a specific application, we do not collect personal user data on external servers.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck stores website URLs entered by the user locally on the device for app functionality. The app is intended to operate without transmitting that user-entered monitoring list to external company-operated servers.

Third-Party Services

FHWebCheck does not use third-party advertising networks. If any future version introduces analytics, cloud sync, or additional external services, this policy should be updated to reflect that change clearly.

User Control

Users can remove stored URLs directly inside the app. Removing the app from the device also removes locally stored app data, subject to normal iOS device behavior and backup settings.
