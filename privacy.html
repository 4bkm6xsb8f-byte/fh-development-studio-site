Privacy Policy

FH Development Studio builds applications that prioritize user privacy and minimal data collection.

Data Collection

Our applications do not collect personal user data unless explicitly stated within the app.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck stores user-provided website URLs locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services in FHWebCheck.

User Control

Users may delete their data at any time by removing content within the app or uninstalling the app.
