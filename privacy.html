Privacy Policy

Clear and minimal data practices.

FH Development Studio explains data practices in plain language and aims to collect only what is reasonably needed to operate products, respond to requests, and support users responsibly.

Overview

This policy applies to FH Development Studio websites and applications unless a product includes a separate, product-specific privacy notice that explains different data practices.

Privacy Approach

FH Development Studio is built around practical software, minimal collection, and straightforward communication. Where possible, products are designed to work with limited data handling and without unnecessary third-party services, advertising technology, or invasive tracking.

Information You Share With Us

If you contact FH Development Studio by email or through the inquiry form, we may receive the information you choose to include, such as your name, email address, company name, project details, screenshots, device context, and support notes. That information is used only to respond to your inquiry, provide support, or evaluate a potential engagement.

Data Collection

FH Development Studio is designed around minimal data handling. Unless explicitly stated within a product, we do not collect personal information on company-operated external servers beyond what is necessary to support communication, product function, or service continuity.

How Information Is Used

Information shared through support or project inquiries may be used to answer questions, troubleshoot issues, improve product behavior, and communicate about requested services. FH Development Studio does not sell personal information and does not use support inquiries as a source for unrelated marketing outreach.

Legal and Operational Basis

When information is provided directly by a user, it is processed only as needed to respond to that request, provide support, maintain service continuity, or communicate about a requested business inquiry.

FHWebCheck

FHWebCheck stores website URLs entered by the user locally on the device to provide the app's functionality. The app is intended to operate without sending that user-managed monitoring list to company-operated servers, which helps keep the product lightweight and privacy-conscious by default.

FHCams

FHCams is designed to present camera feeds through a cleaner interface while working with a range of camera makes, models, and installation ages. Support requests may include camera names, property labels, screenshots, app settings, and device context when a user chooses to share that information for troubleshooting. That information is used only to investigate the issue, improve product quality, or respond to the request.

Third-Party Services

FH Development Studio products are not built around third-party advertising networks. If a future product version adds analytics, cloud sync, camera-vendor integrations, or another external service, this policy should be updated to describe that change clearly, including what data is involved and why the service is needed.

Website and Hosting Considerations

Like most websites, basic technical information such as browser type, device type, referrer details, or server request data may be processed by the hosting platform or infrastructure providers to keep the site available and secure. FH Development Studio may also record page-view events and form submissions needed to understand demand, route inquiries, and improve support response quality. FH Development Studio does not use this site as a vehicle for invasive tracking, advertising-based profiling, or unnecessary background collection.

Third-Party Infrastructure

Website hosting, DNS, content delivery, email delivery, and related infrastructure may involve established third-party providers. Those providers may process limited technical data required to deliver and secure the service, subject to their own operational policies.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect business communications and support information, but no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure in every circumstance. Sensitive information should not be sent through public forms unless it is necessary for support.

User Control

Users can manage or remove stored URLs within the app. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to device behavior, operating system settings, and backup configuration.

Privacy Requests and Questions

If you have a question about how information is handled, or want clarification about a specific product, contact support@fhdevelopmentstudio.com and include enough context for us to identify the relevant app, service, or page involved.

Data Retention

Support emails and inquiry messages may be retained for a reasonable period to maintain support continuity, document prior decisions, and respond to follow-up requests. Website inquiry records and support submissions may also be retained in the FH Development Studio admin workspace for operational continuity. Information is not kept longer than reasonably needed for those purposes.

Children's Privacy

FH Development Studio services are not intentionally directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website or our general support channels.

Policy Updates

This policy may be revised when products, services, or data practices change. Updates will be reflected on this page using the last updated date shown below.
