FHWebCheck Help

Why is a site showing offline?

Temporary connectivity or server issues can cause false offline readings. Recheck after a short period.

How often are checks performed?

Checks occur when the app is opened and periodically in the background, depending on iOS system scheduling.

Notifications not working?

Ensure notifications are enabled for the app in iOS settings.

Typical response time: 1–2 business days.
