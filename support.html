FHWebCheck FAQs

Why is my site showing offline?

A website may appear offline because of temporary hosting issues, network routing problems, SSL or DNS issues, or short-lived server outages. Try checking again after a few minutes.

How often are checks done?

FHWebCheck checks sites when the app is opened and may perform background activity based on iOS scheduling behavior. Actual background timing is controlled in part by the operating system.

Not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled for FHWebCheck in iPhone Settings. Also confirm that Focus modes, summary delivery, or notification permissions are not suppressing alerts.

Typical response time: 1–2 business days.
